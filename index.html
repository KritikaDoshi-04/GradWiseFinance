🎓 College Finance Assistant

Choose a calculator below:

🏦 Net Cost Calculator

Use Case: Determine the actual cost of college after aid and expected contributions.

💡 Tips: Apply for FAFSA early, and consider both federal and private scholarships.

📊 Loan Estimator

Use Case: Understand monthly payments and long-term cost of loans.

💡 Tips: Pay more than the minimum and choose federal loans first due to lower rates.

💳 Student Budget Builder

Use Case: Track monthly income and expenses to avoid overspending.

💡 Tips: Use budgeting apps and track subscriptions to stay within your limits.

🛍️ SmartSpend Analyzer

Use Case: Identify overspending patterns using bank + card summaries.

💡 Tips: Categorize your purchases and cut non-essential spending.

💡 CampusSaver

Use Case: Save money by discovering local campus-specific discounts.

💡 Tips: Always ask stores if they offer student discounts before you pay!

📥 Aid & Refund Tracker

Use Case: Monitor disbursed aid and leftover refund checks.

💡 Tips: Use refund wisely — not for luxury, but for essentials or saving.

🧠 Debt Decoder

Use Case: Understand the total debt left and how long until repayment ends.

💡 Tips: Consider income-based repayment plans if your income is low.

📈 Starter Salary Planner

Use Case: Estimate how much you’ll earn after graduation by location and major.

💡 Tips: Use LinkedIn Salary & Glassdoor to research realistic starting salaries.

📉 Credit Score Coach

Use Case: Learn to maintain and build a good credit score in college.

💡 Tips: Pay full balance monthly and avoid unnecessary hard inquiries.

📚 Textbook Cost Estimator

Use Case: Estimate how much you'll spend on textbooks each term.

💡 Tips: Use online rentals or check campus libraries for free copies.
