Tips:

Spending Analyzer

Tips:
  • Follow the 50-30-20 budgeting rule (Needs-Wants-Savings).
  • Keep needs below 50% of income for balance.
  • Limit wants to 30% to avoid overspending.
  • Prioritize savings for financial security.
  • Regularly review spending categories.
  • Identify and cut discretionary spending when needed.
  • Use spending analysis to inform your budget planning.

Debt Decoder

Tips:

Credit Coach

Learn how to build and maintain good credit:

  • Pay your bills on time every month to build a positive payment history.
  • Keep your credit utilization ratio below 30% of your credit limit.
  • Maintain older credit accounts to lengthen your credit history.
  • Avoid opening too many new credit accounts in a short period.
  • Regularly check your credit report for errors and fraud.
  • Limit hard inquiries by applying for credit only when necessary.
  • Use a mix of credit types responsibly, like credit cards and loans.
